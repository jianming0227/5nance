Why Financial Planning Matters

Emergency Buffer

A safety net covering 3–6 months of living expenses, kept in liquid accounts for unexpected events like job loss or medical bills.

Systematic repayment plans to eliminate high-interest debt within 3–5 years while preserving credit health.

Goal-Based Investing

Building a diversified portfolio aligned with specific timelines and risk tolerance for goals like a home or retirement.

Retirement Readiness

Consistent contributions to tax-advantaged accounts targeting 10–15% of income to ensure lifelong financial independence.
