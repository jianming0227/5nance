How to Use the ROI Calculator

Step 1: Enter Your Initial Investment

Start by entering the amount you plan to invest initially. You can either type the amount or use the slider to select a value.

Step 2: Set Interest Rate & Duration

Enter the expected annual interest rate and how long you plan to keep your investment.

Step 3: Add Contributions (Optional)

If you plan to make regular contributions to your investment, enable this option and specify the amount and frequency.

Step 4: View & Export Results

Click Calculate to see detailed results with interactive charts. You can export the results as a PDF report.
